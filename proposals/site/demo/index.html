01🌐Get found, on Google and in AI answersTraffic
It starts with architecture: a hub-and-spoke system where every page owns one job and one query, so your own pages never compete with each other (the number-one reason local SEO stalls).
A core service hub for every service, targeting pure service intent and serving as the topical authority for that service across your whole area.
An area hub for every city you serve, the page that wins "[service] [city]", not your overworked homepage.
City-service spokes for the long tail, "[service] in [city]" pages, built only where you have real local proof, never templated.
A separate commercial silo for B2B, plus schema and internal links that follow a strict matrix so authority flows the right way.
46%
Why this works: Nearly half of Google searches are local, and AI Overviews now sit on top of them. A complete, non-cannibalizing structure is what gets you into both.

02⭐Build trust before they ever callTrust
A stranger decides in about five seconds. The top 1% put the trust signals right where they land:
The owner's face, front and center, a real photo of the owner with a name and signature, so visitors see a person, not a faceless company.
Phone number and quote form above the fold, both visible the instant the page loads, no scrolling to take action.
A rating callout up top, "rated best in the area" or the star rating, stated plainly next to the headline.
Real personality and local voice, copy that sounds like the business and names the actual town, not a swapped-out template.
Trust badges in view, the credentials your trade is judged on (licensed where it applies, insured, bonded), plus 24/7 or free-estimate callouts, as a strip near the top.
Real photos of real work, the actual crew and completed jobs throughout. Never stock.
★ 4.9
Why this works: Reviews are the number-one driver of map-pack rank. More of them, more recent, beats a competitor on ad spend alone.
03📞Turn the click into a booked jobConversion
A great-looking site that doesn't convert is wasted. The new edge is AI doing the answering and follow-up around the clock:
An AI chatbot that answers instantly, it handles the common questions day or night, so a visitor never leaves unanswered.
Automated email and text follow-up, every lead gets an instant reply and gentle reminders, so nothing slips through the cracks.
Speed to lead, on autopilot, the moment a form hits, you're alerted and the customer is acknowledged. The first to respond wins.
Frictionless forms, only the fields that matter, enough to qualify the job without scaring the buyer off.
Phone numbers everywhere, tap-to-call in the header, the hero, and a sticky mobile bar. Never more than a thumb away.
Trust signals at the decision points, reviews and badges placed right next to the buttons that book the job.
78%
Why this works: Most jobs go to the first contractor to call back. A fast site plus fast follow-up is the difference.
