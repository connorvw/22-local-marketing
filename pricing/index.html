A straight note on ad spend.

Results vary based on competition, market, and budget. Google Ads spend is not included in the management fee — it's paid directly to Google. For most home service markets we recommend at least $1,500 / month in ad budget to see meaningful return. We'll set expectations honestly on the strategy call.

Honest timeline expectations.

SEO compounds. Paid is faster. Here's roughly how the first year shakes out.

Months 1–3
Early momentum.
Foundation goes in — new site, fresh GBP, tracking, baseline content. Leads start flowing from LSA, GBP, and paid (if on Growth or Dominate) while organic indexes.
Months 3–6
Noticeable growth.
Service and location pages start ranking. Reviews stack up. Organic traffic kicks in. Paid efficiency improves as data accrues and we cut what doesn't work.
Months 6–12+
Market domination.
You're in the map pack. You're answering questions buyers ask before they call. Organic dominates lead volume, paid plays defense, cost per lead drops.
Accelerated track
Want results faster? Growth and Dominate include managed Google Ads from day one. Paid lead flow starts in week one while SEO works in the background. Most clients see their first qualified lead inside 14 days.
