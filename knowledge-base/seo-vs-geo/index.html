Why this matters now

People are asking AI for recommendations before they even open Google.

“Find me a plumber in Wake Forest” goes to ChatGPT first for a growing chunk of buyers. If you’re not cited there, you’re invisible in that conversation.

The good news

GEO is built on the same foundation as SEO.

Real content. Real authority. Clean structure. Reviews and citations from trusted sources. If your SEO is right, your GEO is mostly already right.

The takeaway

Stop chasing trends. Build a real business online.

Solid site, deep content, honest reputation. That ranks on Google and gets cited by AI. The fundamentals don’t change.
