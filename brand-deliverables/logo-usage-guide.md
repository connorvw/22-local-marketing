# 22 Local Marketing Logo Usage Guide

## Primary Logo

The primary logo is the Blaze rounded square mark plus the Sora wordmark:

22 Local Marketing

Use the full logo whenever there is enough horizontal space.

## Mark

The standalone orange rounded square is reserved for:

- favicons
- social avatars
- app-style icons
- small watermarks
- pattern/detail moments

Do not add symbols, initials, map pins, gradients, outlines, or texture inside the square.

## Clear Space

Minimum clear space around the full logo should equal at least 2x the width of the orange square.

For the standalone mark, clear space should equal at least 1x the mark width on all sides.

## Minimum Size

- Full horizontal logo: 140 px wide minimum on screen.
- Small horizontal logo: 96 px wide minimum on screen.
- Standalone mark: 16 px minimum on screen.
- Print mark: 0.15 in minimum.

## Approved Color Versions

1. Primary: Blaze mark + Onyx wordmark on Bone or White.
2. Reversed: Blaze mark + Blaze or White wordmark on Onyx.
3. One-color: Onyx mark + Onyx wordmark when color is unavailable.

## Backgrounds

Best backgrounds:

- Bone
- White
- Onyx

Acceptable:

- simple photography with a quiet area behind the logo
- solid brand-color panels

Avoid:

- busy photos behind the logo
- low contrast color combinations
- gradients behind the logo
- placing the logo inside decorative containers

## Incorrect Usage

Do not:

- stretch or skew the logo
- rotate the mark
- change the font
- use colors outside the brand palette
- add drop shadows or glows
- place icons inside the orange square
- use the square as a bullet everywhere
- recreate the logo manually when an SVG is available
