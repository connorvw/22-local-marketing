# 22 Local Marketing Photography And Art Direction

## Photography Role

Photography should make the brand feel real, local, and service-business specific. It should support trust and operational competence, not look like generic agency stock.

## Preferred Subjects

- actual home service crews
- work trucks and wrapped vehicles
- technicians on real job sites
- hands working with tools
- finished work
- local neighborhoods
- business owners in practical environments
- before and after field documentation
- screenshots of maps, reviews, call reports, and dashboards

## Style

- natural daylight
- crisp contrast
- warm but not nostalgic
- real people, real tools, real locations
- slightly editorial cropping
- plenty of negative space for type

## Avoid

- fake handshake stock photos
- abstract laptop marketing scenes
- overly polished corporate portraits
- dark moody construction images
- heavy filters
- AI-looking tradespeople
- generic city skylines
- decorative 3D icons

## Image Treatments

Use images in rectangular blocks with 6-8 px border radius. Add Onyx 1 px borders when images sit on White or Bone.

Use Blaze as:

- a frame
- a label
- a small corner detail
- a data highlight

Do not wash full photos in orange unless used for a campaign-specific graphic.

## Social Photo Rules

For 1:1 social posts:

- preserve 8-10% safe margin
- place text over quiet negative space only
- use one image per post unless creating a carousel
- keep the orange frame visible
- use short, direct captions
