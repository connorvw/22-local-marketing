# 22 Local Marketing Ad Creative Guidelines

## Ad Strategy

The creative should make a practical promise and show a believable mechanism. Use direct language and strong contrast.

## Default Formats

- Feed square: 1080 x 1080
- Story/Reels cover: 1080 x 1920
- LinkedIn image: 1200 x 1200 or 1200 x 627
- Display ad starting sizes: 300 x 250, 728 x 90, 160 x 600, 300 x 600

## Creative Types

### Problem/Solution

Headline: Your website is leaking local leads.

Support: Fix the pages customers see before they call.

CTA: Get a local audit

### KPI Proof

Headline: +32% more qualified calls.

Support: Landing page rebuild + local SEO structure.

CTA: See the system

### Service Offer

Headline: Local SEO for home service companies.

Support: Maps, service pages, reviews, and tracking.

CTA: Book a call

### Audit Offer

Headline: Know why competitors rank above you.

Support: Get a practical local visibility audit.

CTA: Start with an audit

## Layout Rules

- One dominant headline.
- One supporting line.
- One CTA.
- Use a Blaze frame or rule.
- Keep logo small.
- Avoid tiny paragraphs.
- Do not overuse icons.

## Color Rules

Best combinations:

- Bone background, Onyx type, Blaze accent.
- White background, Onyx type, Blaze accent.
- Onyx background, White or Blaze type, Blaze accent.

Avoid:

- orange backgrounds with orange accents
- low contrast text
- heavy gradients
- more than one non-brand color

## Copy Rules

Use:

- specific outcomes
- clear pain points
- service-area language
- lead quality language

Avoid:

- broad marketing claims
- jargon-heavy ad tech language
- exaggerated guarantees
- "dominate your market" style copy
