# 22 Local Marketing Voice Guide

## Brand Voice

22 Local Marketing sounds like a sharp growth operator for home service businesses. The voice is direct, practical, confident, and revenue-aware.

It should never sound like a vague marketing agency, startup hype machine, or motivational quote account.

## Voice Principles

1. Lead with outcomes.
   Use calls, booked jobs, rankings, reviews, service areas, conversion rate, and revenue.

2. Make the system visible.
   Explain the mechanism behind the result: website structure, local SEO, landing pages, ads, tracking, reviews, GBP optimization.

3. Keep the language concrete.
   Prefer "more qualified calls from your service area" over "unlock your potential."

4. Speak to serious operators.
   The audience is busy and skeptical. Respect that.

## Message Pillars

- Local visibility: Get found where customers are searching.
- Trust: Turn reviews, proof, and service-area relevance into conversion.
- Speed: Make it easy for customers to call, book, and choose you.
- Measurement: Tie marketing to leads, calls, and booked jobs.
- Growth systems: Build repeatable acquisition instead of one-off campaigns.
