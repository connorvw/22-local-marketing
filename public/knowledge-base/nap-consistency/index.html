NAP Consistency
Search Engine Optimization

What is NAP consistency?

A small thing that quietly kills rankings for home service companies who ignore it.

The definition

NAP stands for three things. They have to match exactly everywhere.

N
Name
Your exact business name, every time. “ABC Home Services LLC” is not the same as “ABC Home Services.”
A
Address
Same street, suite, city, state, ZIP. “St” is not the same as “Street.”
P
Phone
One number, formatted the same way. Pick one. Use it everywhere.
Why it matters

Google can’t physically visit you. It verifies you’re real by cross-checking your NAP across the web.

Hundreds of small mismatches add up to one big signal: “This business isn’t legitimate, or isn’t active.” Rankings drop. The map pack passes you over. Your competitors with cleaner data win.

Where it lives

Every place your business is mentioned online.

Google Business Profile
source of truth
Your website
footer / contact / schema
Yelp, Bing, Apple Maps
the big three
Industry directories
Angi / HomeAdvisor / Thumbtack
Local citations
BBB / chamber / news
Social profiles
Facebook / Instagram / LinkedIn
The fix

Do it once. Maintain it forever.

  • 01
    Audit
    Run your business through Whitespark, BrightLocal, or a manual sweep. Document every mismatch.
  • 02
    Correct
    Start with GBP and your own website. Then work down the directory list, top to bottom.
  • 03
    Lock the format
    Pick one exact NAP. Write it down. That’s the only version anyone uses, forever.
  • 04
    Maintain
    Every new directory listing uses the locked format. Every business card, invoice, vehicle wrap. Same NAP.
