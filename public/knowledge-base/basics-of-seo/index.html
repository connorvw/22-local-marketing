The Basics of SEO.

How to get started with ranking on Google.

What it is

SEO is how you get found when someone Googles “[your service] near me.”

Three things decide if you show up: your site, your content, and your reputation.

The playing field

Where your business can show up.

Five spots, one search. Knowing which is which is step one.

The three pillars

What Google actually rewards.

  • 01
    Technical foundation
    Fast site, mobile-friendly, every page indexable. Google has to read you before it can rank you.
  • 02
    Content depth
    Every service you offer and every city you serve, each with a full page.
  • 03
    Authority signals
    Reviews, citations, backlinks. Trust Google can verify from outside your own site.
The blind spot

Most home service companies have a 5-page website and 12 reviews. Then they wonder why they don’t rank.

Page count alone won’t fix it. Depth plus trust will.

The honest part

SEO is a 6 to 12 month investment.

Anyone promising results in 30 days is renting you ads and calling it SEO.
