The 5-Minute Rule

The 5-minute rule.

How fast a lead loses value when you don’t respond.

The decay curve

Studies on millions of inbound leads agree.

5 min
Peak Window
Respond inside 5 minutes and you’re 100x more likely to qualify the lead.
30 min
First Drop
Past 30 minutes, qualification rate falls off a cliff.
24 hrs
Effectively Dead
Most leads have moved on or hired someone else.
Why the decay is so fast

When someone has a problem, they want it solved now.

A homeowner with a tree on their roof, a leaking water heater, a stopped-up drain. They’re not shopping at leisure. They’re in panic mode. Five minutes later they’re on the phone with someone who picked up.

How to stay inside 5 minutes

What actually works.

  • 01
    Automated instant text
    Triggered the moment the form submits. “Got your request. Calling in 2 min.”
  • 02
    Phone alerts, not email
    Email is a graveyard. SMS or push notification with audible alert.
  • 03
    Voicemail forwards to text
    If you miss the call, the transcript goes to your phone immediately.
  • 04
    Backup answerer for off-hours
    Even a $200/month service is cheaper than the leads you’d lose.
The takeaway

The lead is most valuable the second it lands.

Every minute after that, it’s worth less. By tomorrow, it’s gone.
