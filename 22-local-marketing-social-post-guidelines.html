Default Format

Design posts at 1080 x 1080 px by default. Keep the important content inside a 10% safe zone so the frame, captions, and platform UI never crowd the message.

Ratio

Default to 1:1 square. Use 1080 x 1080 px for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and general feed posts.

Margins

Use 8% inner padding. Headlines can be large, but never touch the accent frame.

Logo

Use the orange rounded square plus wordmark in small footer or header positions. Keep it quiet.

Voice

Short, direct, results-minded. Avoid hype. Sound sharp, useful, and grounded.

Accent Frames

Every post should use one frame treatment. The frame is the brand signal: orange, geometric, and restrained.

Design Specs

These are the guardrails. Staying inside them keeps the feed consistent without making every post identical.

Typography

  • Font: Sora only.
  • Headlines: 700-800 weight, tight line-height.
  • Labels: uppercase, 0.12em letter spacing.
  • Body: 400-600 weight, Slate or light opacity on dark.

Composition

  • Use one dominant message per post.
  • Keep content aligned left in most cases.
  • Use the brand square as a small signal, not a large decoration.
  • Avoid centered motivational poster layouts.

Frame Use

  • Corner frame is the default.
  • Left rule supports longer copy.
  • Top band is for high-priority announcements.
  • Frame color is always Blaze.

Color Palette

Orange does the talking. Bone, White, and Onyx carry the layout. Slate is for supporting text only.
